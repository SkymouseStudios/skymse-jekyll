The Process

Good advertising tells a story, educating the viewer.

1. Select the audience.

Identify the primary campaign audience and their motivation.

2. Create the content.

Develop campaign creative content suited for your audience.

3. Put it in motion.

Launch the campaign on email, social, and website channels.

4. Track the response.

Use platform insights and user testing to deduce user motivations.

5. Make it better.

The best data you can get is the actions your users are taking.
