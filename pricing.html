Website OptiPack

Websites should be fast.™

You may have just redone your website or maybe it’s been a few years and you’re not quite ready to redo your entire site yet. You should always be checking on your website ensuring that it is still providing a digital headquarters for your company. The internet never sleeps. Here are three elements of optimizing your website. Think of it as a "spring cleaning for the web."

Speed

Many users are accessing your site via mobile phone, which means they are much less patient with page-load times. Very often carless developers or template-based website can be the cause of a bloated website. Not only will this affect if your users stay on your site, it will affect how search engines rank your site.

SEO

A quick optimization of your website can greatly improve how you show up in search results on google. Some improvements include adding an SSL certificate, writing copy that is easy to read and making sure your site is up to par with Google’s latest search algorithm standards.

Content

Web standards change over time and there are always new and better ways to make your site load faster and ensure your website is actually making you money. Ensuring your images are compressed, minify, delete old pages.
