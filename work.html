What Does Skymouse Do?

Skymouse Studios a web design company based out of Indianapolis, working to bring life back into websites. We love to travel and explore the unknown, so we never limit ourselves to one location.

We were born on the principals of keeping in touch with our clients, providing exceptional service and getting down to the nitty-gritty when a site needs to be made.

The New Barbarian

Robert Storey needed a platform to launch his new lifestyle brand for men. We worked on creating marketing materials and a site that captured the raw flavor of his brand while driving site visitors to download his new workout product.

St. Martin's Catholic Church

St. Martin's had been suffering with a confusing website that failed to capture the essence of the parish and the events that were often taking place. We met with the pastor and put together a site that was easy to navigate and featured professional photos from around the church campus.
